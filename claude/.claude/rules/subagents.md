Subagents (Agent tool): keep read-mostly fan-out (research, search, review) on a cheaper/faster model; reserve the session's own model for genuinely hard reasoning, and note why when you do. Use whatever cheap tier your backend offers - on Claude, `sonnet` for fan-out and `haiku` for trivial lookups; on another backend, its equivalent (use the full model id, not an alias the backend won't recognize).

Never poll background agents or tasks with sleep loops: end the turn instead - the completion notification resumes you automatically. A sleeping turn costs a full model call with thinking; the notification is free.
